# D&D 5e (2024) Mechanic Rules

## Proficiency Bonus
- Level 1-4: +2
- Level 5-8: +3
- Level 9-12: +4
- Level 13-16: +5
- Level 17-20: +6

## Stats & Modifiers
- Formula: `Math.floor((stat - 10) / 2)`
- Stats over 20 are NOT allowed unless the background/race/magic item specifically says so.

## Health Calculations
- Hit Dice: 1d12 (Barbarian), 1d10 (Fighter/Paladin), etc.
- Short Rest: Roll Hit Dice and add CON modifier.
- Long Rest: Reset current HP to max. Reset Half total Hit Dice.

## Combat Actions
- Bonus Action: Only ONE per turn.
- Reaction: Only ONE per round.
- Advantage: Roll two d20 and take the HIGH.
- Disadvantage: Roll two d20 and take the LOW.
